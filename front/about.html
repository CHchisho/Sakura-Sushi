About Sakura Sushi

A journey through authentic Japanese flavors

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Sakura Sushi began as a dream to bring authentic Japanese cuisine to the heart of Helsinki. Our master chef, trained in Tokyo for over 15 years, combines traditional techniques with the freshest local ingredients.

Every dish is crafted with precision and passion, honoring the centuries-old traditions of Japanese culinary art while embracing modern innovation.
